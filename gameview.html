welcome to Control Alt Steal! here's how the game works:

  1. Pick your level based on how challenging you want it to be
  2. You will be given a detailed utline of what happened at the crime scene
  3. Find the clue and look at the crime scence carefully to not miss a hint
  4. Based on the clues guess who you think the murder was
  5. Once youve guessed it right you will be able to move onto the next level
  6. Make sure to click on objects at the crime scene to give you clues/hints
